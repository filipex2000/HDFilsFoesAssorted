Made by CarThief.
Usage: Use as you like for non-commercial purposes. Credit is optional but appreciated.
Notes: Some firing frames might be Truecolour PNG. Firing brightmaps are Truecolour PNG.

-ArmorGunner: The 'Gunner', but with a shield.
-Grenade:     Zombie with shoulder-fired rocket/grenade launcher.
-Gunner:      Big zombie with a machinegun.
-Hoarder:     Big zombie with backpack and rocket launcher.
-Impactor:    Big zombie with mini-missile gun.
-Insurgent:   Zombie with bandana and grenade launcher.
-Kamikaze:    Big zombie with backpack full of TNT.
-Looter:      Zombie with rifle and backpack.
-Medic:       Zombie with a gun that can heal and shoot.
-Pipebomb:    Dodge-rolling pipebomb-throwing zombie.
-Riot:        Big riot surpressing zombie with tazer and riot shield.
